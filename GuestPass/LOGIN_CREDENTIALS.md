## ⚠️ Important Changes

### What Changed?
- ❌ **OLD:** Everyone used the same password "Grandcity123"
- ✅ **NEW:** Each user has their own unique password

### Why This Change?
- 🔒 **Better Security:** Individual passwords are more secure
- 📊 **Accountability:** We can track who does what
- 🛡️ **Protection:** If one password is compromised, others remain safe

### Login Protection
- After **5 failed login attempts**, your account will be **locked for 30 minutes**
- Your session will **automatically expire after 24 hours**
- If you see "Access token required" - just log in again

---

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Invalid email or password"
- ✅ Check you selected the correct role
- ✅ If Executive, verify you selected your name correctly
- ✅ Make sure password is exactly as shown above (case-sensitive)
- ✅ No extra spaces before or after password

### "Account is locked"
- ⏰ Wait 30 minutes after failed login attempts
- 📞 Contact admin if urgent: admin@grandcity.pk

### "Access token required"
- 🔄 Your session expired - just log in again
- This happens after 24 hours of being logged in

### Can't find your name in dropdown?
- Make sure you selected "Executive" role first
- The executive list loads from the database
- If still not showing, contact admin

## 📱 First Time Login

1. Open the Guest Pass System
2. You'll see the login screen with:
   - **Role dropdown** (Executive, Staff, Guard, Receptionist, Admin)
   - **Executive dropdown** (if you selected Executive role)
   - **Password field**
3. Select your role and name (if executive)
4. Enter your password from the table above
5. Click "Login to Dashboard"
6. You're in! Everything else works the same way
